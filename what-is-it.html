Introducing VHS

Verdict Hub System (VHS) provides trailblazing, AI-driven surveys that enable trial teams to anticipate how evidence and arguments central to your case will be evaluated by venue-specific jurors. Our surveys empower attorneys to test and refine trial strategies with unprecedented speed, depth, and clarity. VHS is built by a multidisciplinary team—including nationally renowned trial consultant Robert Hirschhorn—bringing together more than a century of award-winning expertise in jury and trial consultation and AI innovation.

How It Works

Three simple steps:

1. Submit Case Materials

Provide fact-intense pleadings, deposition excerpts, case summaries, and other relevant documents.

2. Specify Your Trial Venue

VHS models juror decision-making based on that particular jurisdiction through the use of digital jurors.

3. Receive Insightful Findings

Get comprehensive written findings within three to five business days.
