What Is VerdictHub.ai?

Your AI-Powered Case Co-Pilot from Filing to Verdict

Introducing VHS

VHS—short for Verdict Hub System—is your AI-powered case co-pilot. It helps you simulate how real jurors from your venue might perceive liability, evidence, and damages—using jurisdiction-specific digital juror personas grounded in behavioral realism.

Unlike traditional jury research such as focus groups, or mock trials, which are expensive, static, and limited to one or two time points, VHS is agile and scalable. You can VHS your case early, midstream, or right before trial—each time generating targeted insights to inform your strategy.

VHS provides trailblazing, AI-driven surveys that enable trial teams to anticipate how evidence and arguments central to your case will be evaluated by venue-specific jurors. Our surveys empower attorneys to test and refine trial strategy with unprecedented speed, depth, and clarity.

How VHS Works

VHS is built by a multidisciplinary team—including nationally renowned trial consultant Robert Hirschhorn—bringing together more than a century of award-winning expertise in jury and trial consultation and AI innovation.

1. Submit Case Materials

Provide fact-intense pleadings, deposition excerpts, case summaries, and other relevant documents.

2. Specify Your Trial Venue

VHS models juror decision-making based on that particular jurisdiction through the use of digital jurors.

3. Receive Insightful Findings

Get comprehensive written findings within three business days to inform your trial strategy.

The Technology Behind VHS

We use AI large language models (LLMs) trained on trillions of datasets—including legal documents, human behavior, and decision-making logic. VHS processes your case materials to extract key facts, claims, defenses, and disputes—while staying grounded in your actual documents. We maintain strict confidentiality and ensure your data is secure.

Digital Juror Personas

Our proprietary patent pending technology allows us to build Agentic AI based personas that reflect the actual demographic makeup of your trial venue. We generate juror responses that are dynamic, grounded, and behaviorally rich—more like how people actually think.

Custom Survey Instruments

We generate a custom survey instrument, tailored to your case, including legal standards, fact-specific hypotheticals, open-ended reasoning prompts, fault allocations, damage modeling, and more.

AI Simulation

Our AI Agents simulate how real jurors reason—then we analyze the results at both the individual and population level to provide you with actionable insights.

Comprehensive Analysis

Behind the scene, VHS is using a completely new patent pending paradigm that goes beyond traditional statistical methods for research, providing deeper and more nuanced insights.
